The Beache sits on a chair  
Sheppas ignore her like air  
So she thinks to make a blend  
Made of sheppas, a new trend

The sheppas run for their lives  
Into hidey holes in hives  
Beache bangs hard on the walls  
Which drop like heavy rainfalls
