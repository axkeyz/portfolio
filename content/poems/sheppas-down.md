---
title: "Sheppas down"
date: 2020-04-20T16:00:18+12:00
draft: false
---

Sheppas scrabble day and night,  
Diving right in with delight.  
Cuby gives sheppas a frown -  
Time for sheppas to go down.

All gifted back as slaves,  
Sheppas sigh and carve their graves.  
Beache smirks with boastful glee,  
Watching sheppas scram and flee.

But this time Beache “plays fair”,  
Giving sheppas work to share:  
As long as all work is done,  
Extra time is left for fun.

Sadly there are selfish punks,  
Lazing about, being drunks.  
These sheppas are the victims,  
Of the Beache’s fearsome whims....

But there are the tragic few,  
Bullied as a thing to chew,  
No particular reason,  
Just for some made up treason.
