Cuby lounges back in bed,
Nothing sticking in her head.
Woken up by drumming rain,
Now trying to sleep in vain.

Reading Beache’s sad email,
Cuby takes a big inhale.
She hopes the coat is alright,
Not taken without a fight.

I will try to hurry back.
When we arrive, hit the track,
Speed towards your waiting friend.
This story needs a good end.
