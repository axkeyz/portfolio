He fumes to "friends" in the group,  
They laugh and call him a dupe.  
"You fools are so mean!" He cries,  
All alone, just filled with sighs.

Then a kind Beache appears,  
Behind her back, hiding shears,  
"Beache comfort me!" He whines,  
No doubt missing all the signs....
