Next door to the sheppa shop,
Opens a store, selling chop.
Where do parts originate?
From sheppas out on a date.

It becomes the new food trend,
Sheppas' spirits start to bend.
They serve Beache head to toe,
Fearing to become her foe.

Beache sits back on her chair,
Sitting with a queenly air.
Sheppas pool around her feet,
Sighing/crying in defeat.

At the store of sheppa chop,
Slowly sales start to drop,
Closure becomes imminent,
Sheppa cheer is evident.
