## Background

Based on a game called Host no Abunai Sekai (ホストのアブナイ世界), which roughly translates to "The Dangerous World of Hosts". I didn't understand a word of it, but it was surprisingly fun, even if you didn't understand a word of it. The story starts with the player (you) getting scammed by a loan shark. Then you start running a host club where you scam other women in order to pay back that loan. There are a number of upgrades that help you earn more money over time. The expressions of the ones who got scammed by you are absolutely priceless.

But the game wasn't really catered for long-term playing and wasn't hardcore enough. My game will be a bit more hardcore than that. The loan you have will now have a 50% interest rate per week (I plan to give all crippling depression as you give away your money every week). There'll be a more intelligent customer and employee host AI which you may benefit from, if you don't fall into the traps. Maybe they'll be a 98% success cap, just to mess with you.

## Objectives

- Replicate the basic mechanics of Host no Abunai Sekai.
- Add mechanics to increase its difficulty and nastiness.

## Approach

- **Game Engine**: Godot. Simple, light, great for 2D games and my 4GB RAM slug of a laptop can handle it.
- **Deployment**: Offline mobile game. Autosaves to local cache.
